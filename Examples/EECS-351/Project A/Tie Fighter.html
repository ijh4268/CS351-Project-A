Instructions

To start and stop the rotation of the checkpoint (bottom left figure), press the button.

To increase or decrease the rotation speed of the Tie Fighter's wings, use the corresponding buttons.

Click on the Tie Fighter and drag the mouse to move it around the canvas.

Clicking on the canvas will stop the rotation of the arm (bottom right).

Use w,a,s,d keys to move the arm around the canvas.

Stretch the base of the checkpoint by entering a value between 0.5 and 4.0 into the entry box.
