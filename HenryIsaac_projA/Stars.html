Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Instructions

  1. Click and drag to turn the 3D Hexagram.
  2. Double-click and drag to move the 3D Hexagram on the canvas.
  3. Use the "W", "A", "S", and "D" keys to move the Pyramid on the canvas.
  4. You can input an angle into the submission box to change the angle of the Pyramid and Snake.
  5. You can use the "Spin" buttons to increase or decrease the rate the Pyramid spins.
  6. You can use the "Run/Stop" button to pause or run the animation.
